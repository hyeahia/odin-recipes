Brownies

Description

Looking for the best brownie recipe on the internet? You've come to the right place! These rich, fudgy brownies are topped with a decadent homemade frosting.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tspn vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 tspn salt
  • 1/4 tspn baking powder
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  3. Melt 1/2 cup butter in a large saucepan.
  4. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
  5. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 25 to 30 minutes.
